# Fractured Reality

A fractured world.  
A broken sky.  
A civilization rebuilt through machinery, energy and forgotten dimensions.

## About

Fractured Reality is a heavily customized Minecraft 1.21.1 modpack focused on:

- ⚙️ Progressive tech evolution
- 🌍 Exploration and dimensional progression
- 📖 Quest-driven experience
- 🧩 Custom recipes and progression systems
- 🚀 Space and end-game automation
- 🎨 Immersive atmosphere and worldbuilding

The modpack is designed around multiple technological eras, guiding the player from primitive mechanical systems to advanced digital networks and interplanetary exploration.

## Features

- Custom quest book progression
- Fully reworked recipes
- Custom Create integration
- Unique resource generation systems
- Atmospheric intro sequences
- Custom textures and visual effects
- Progression-based exploration
- End-game creative objectives
